Welcome

Welcome to the course page for Foundations of Computer and Programming. This course introduces students to the fundamental concepts of computing, algorithmic thinking, and programming principles.

Designed for students across mathematics, computer science, and related disciplines, this course provides the essential toolkit for computational problem-solving. You'll develop not just programming skills, but the analytical mindset characteristic of computer scientists.
